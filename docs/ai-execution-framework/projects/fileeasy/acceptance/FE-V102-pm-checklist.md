# FE-V102 PM Acceptance Checklist

## Goal

Verify that FileEasy has an end-to-end validation result strong enough to drive release-closure planning.

## Checks

1. Validation coverage
- upload page flow was assessed
- upload backend flow was assessed
- admin-page management flow was assessed
- product-boundary checks were included

2. Result quality
- passed scenarios are listed explicitly
- failed scenarios are listed explicitly
- blockers are separated from non-blockers

3. PM usability
- report gives a clear go/no-go recommendation
- any unverified area is clearly called out
- PM can decide whether to launch blocker-fix tasks

## Reject Conditions

- report only gives a vague summary without scenario-level results
- blockers and non-blockers are mixed together
- upload/admin core flows are not covered
- no clear PM handoff recommendation is provided
